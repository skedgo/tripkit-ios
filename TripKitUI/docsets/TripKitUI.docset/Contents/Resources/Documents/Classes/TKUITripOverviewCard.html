  • selectedAlternativeTripCallback

    Set this callback to include a “Show routes” button, which then presents the TKUIRoutingResultsCard and selecting a different trip will trigger this callback.

    Returning true will lead to that trip being displayed as usual in another TKUITripOverviewCard that gets pushed, and returning false will do nothing, i.e., the callback handles displaying it.
